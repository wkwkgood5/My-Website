About

Hello all! My name is Kang Wang. I am a PhD student in the school of Public Health and Health Systems at the University of Waterloo.

My research interests have revolved around creating a Mobile Health platform which could analyze health data with Machine Learning and Deep Learning for remote patient monitoring (RPM) and give personalized recommendations to target people to prevent unnecessary visits to hospital.
